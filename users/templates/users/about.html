About AuthentiCred

Revolutionizing credential verification through blockchain technology and decentralized identity management.

Our Mission

To create a secure, transparent, and efficient ecosystem for digital credential verification, empowering institutions and individuals with trusted, tamper-proof credentials.

Security

Blockchain-based verification ensures credentials are tamper-proof and permanently verifiable.

Transparency

Open and transparent verification process that builds trust between all parties.

Efficiency

Instant verification reduces administrative overhead and speeds up credential validation.

How It Works

1

Issue

Institutions create and issue digital credentials to students or professionals.

2

Store

Credentials are securely stored in users' digital wallets with blockchain anchoring.

3

Share

Users can share credentials via secure links or QR codes with verifiers.

4

Verify

Verifiers can instantly validate credentials against the blockchain.

Key Features

Blockchain Verification

All credentials are anchored on the blockchain for permanent verification.

Decentralized Identity

Users control their own digital identity and credentials.

Instant Verification

Real-time credential verification with immediate results.

QR Code Sharing

Easy credential sharing through QR codes and secure links.

PDF Export

Download credentials as professional PDF documents.

Multi-Role Support

Support for institutions, students, and verifiers.
