Frequently Asked Questions

How does AuthentiCred work?

AuthentiCred uses blockchain technology to create tamper-proof digital credentials. Institutions issue credentials that are anchored on the blockchain, making them permanently verifiable.

Is AuthentiCred secure?

Yes, AuthentiCred uses industry-standard encryption and blockchain technology to ensure the highest level of security for all credentials and user data.

How much does it cost?

AuthentiCred offers flexible pricing plans for institutions. Contact us for a custom quote based on your specific needs and volume.

Can I integrate with existing systems?

Yes, AuthentiCred provides APIs and integration options to work with your existing student information systems and credential management platforms.
